This is the info file for the FBR 3 manual organ, version 2.0 This sample set was produced for use in Hauptwerk software version 2.0, available from Crumhorm Labs

Most of the samples for this organ were taken from the 1966 Schantz organ in the First Baptist Church of Riverside, California. The remaining stops were taken from other venues (which must remain confidential due to agreements with those entities.)

Since this is a fictitious organ, there are no pictures

You may check evensongmusic.net for updates and further samplesets
